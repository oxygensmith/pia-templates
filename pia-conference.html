Public Interest Alberta's 11th annual advocacy conference

This year, we are tackling BOLD advocacy for BIG changes. The conference begins on Thursday, March 30 with an advocacy training and evening keynote and will continue all day Friday, March 31.

Speakers and panelists will dive into topics like the current state of media, experiences in grassroots organizing, and current events.

This year's conference will consider issues in each of the following areas:

  • Education
  • Democracy
  • Post-secondary education
  • Seniors
  • Human services and poverty
  • Child care and early learning
  • Environment

Tickets will go on sale in late February and will be available through this page.  Stay tuned!

If you are having difficulties registering for the conference, please email office@pialberta.org.
